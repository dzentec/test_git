Main Process

We usually build our services around the three step sequence:

  • Proof of concept: here an R&D and feasibility study should be done (which means that product is feasible technically)
  • MVP: in this step we need to move from the proof of concept to the next level of the product maturity (which means that product can demonstrate its core features and they are accepted by the stakeholders)
  • DFM: here product goes through all generic phases starting from EVT to PVT while all product aspects being adjusted to the needs of further mass production
